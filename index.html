Curse of Strahd

A Daggerheart Campaign Frame

The Pitch

The mists have taken you. You find yourselves in Barovia, a land trapped in eternal twilight, ruled by the immortal vampire Count Strahd von Zarovich.

A fortune-teller named Madam Eva reveals your destiny through her tarokka cards—you are meant to face the ancient evil that haunts this land.

Can you break the curse and free Barovia? Or will you become just another ghost wandering the Old Svalich Road?

Campaign Overview

The Land of Barovia

Barovia exists in a demiplane, torn from its original world by mysterious Dark Powers. Deadly mists surround the land. The sun never fully breaks through the clouds, casting everything in perpetual gloom.

Strahd von Zarovich

Once a proud conqueror, Strahd made a terrible pact to gain eternal life. He murdered his brother Sergei and became a vampire, but lost Tatyana forever.

Every generation, Tatyana's soul is reborn. The current incarnation is Ireena Kolyana.

Principles

Player Principles

Embrace the Horror

Barovia is a land of dread and despair. Let your character feel fear and doubt. The darkness makes your courage shine brighter.

Find Light in Darkness

There are people worth saving. Your Hope tokens represent actual hope—use them to create moments that matter.

GM Principles

Make Barovia Feel Alive (and Dead)

Describe the oppressive atmosphere constantly. But also show signs of life struggling to persist.

Play Strahd as Complex, Not Evil

Strahd is a tragic figure. He can be charming, philosophical, even merciful when it suits him.

Custom Mechanics

The Mists of Ravenloft

Deadly fog surrounds Barovia, preventing escape.

Effect:

  • Characters attempting to leave must make a Presence + Instinct roll (Difficulty 18)
  • Failure: Lost in mists, mark 2 Stress per hour

Dim Barovia (Sunlight Rules)

The sun never truly shines in Barovia.

Effect:

  • Daylight does NOT count as sunlight for vampire vulnerabilities
  • Strahd can move about during the day
  • Magical sunlight still affects vampires normally

Fortunes of Ravenloft

Madam Eva performs a tarokka reading determining artifact locations.

The Fated Items:

  • The Tome of Strahd
  • The Sunsword
  • The Holy Symbol of Ravenkind

When a PC discovers a fated item, they gain 3 Hope and clear 1d4 Stress.

Tier 4 Solo - The Ancient Vampire Lord

The ancient vampire lord of Barovia, a tragic figure of immense power and cunning.

Difficulty

20

Thresholds

15/30

HP

8

Attack

+5

Key Features

  • Shapechanger: Transform into bat, wolf, or mist
  • Legendary Resistance: Ignore effects 3 times per session
  • Regeneration: Clear 2 HP at start of turn
  • Heart of Sorrow: Damage transferred to crystal heart
  • Charm: Dominate minds (Difficulty 18)

Weaknesses

  • Cannot enter homes without invitation
  • True sunlight stops regeneration
  • Stake through heart destroys permanently

Character Options

Fitting Ancestries into Barovia

Humans

The most common ancestry. Native Barovians are pale, dark-haired, and haunted.

Fungril

Mushroom folk who grow in damp, decaying places. Their connection to death resonates here.

Communities in Barovia

Wildborne

Those who live in the Svalich Woods. The Keepers of the Feather (wereravens) could be Wildborne.

Order-Bound

Survivors of the Order of the Silver Dragon or the church of the Morninglord.
